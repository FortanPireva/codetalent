• Curated tech jobs — Browse positions from top companies, filtered by role, location, experience level, and tech stack. Frontend, backend, AI, DevOps — we've got you covered.

• One-tap apply — View detailed job descriptions with salary ranges, requirements, and tech stacks. Apply directly with your Talentflow profile.

• Application tracker — Monitor all your applications in one clean dashboard. See real-time status updates as you progress through each hiring stage.

• Developer profile — Showcase your technical skills, connect your GitHub and LinkedIn, and let employers find you. Complete coding assessments to earn AI-verified skill badges.

• Smart notifications — Get alerted when new jobs match your preferences, when application statuses change, and when assessment results are ready.

• Personalized experience — Light mode, dark mode, and customizable notification preferences to make the app your own.
